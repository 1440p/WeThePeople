WeThePeople Privacy Statement

The online voter registration system brought to you by WeThePeople, is committed to protecting the privacy and accuracy of
confidential information to the extent possible, subject to provisions of state and federal law.

Your Data Privacy is Important to us!

Although there are many applications and websites that collect information and other statistics about
their users, we DO collect any personal information about you when you register to vote using this web site at WeThePeople.

This Privacy Statement does not apply to the other local & state government web sites.
You should review the privacy statement posted on local & state government when you visit them.

Type of Information Collected

The data collected from registering users are the following; First & Last name, State Government ID, Social Security Number, and Password.
No addresses, phone numbers, e-mail addresses, IP addresses, nor access dates and times are collected from either registering user or visitor on the WeThePeople website.

But why is this Information collected?

This data collection is required for appropriate verification as a legal registered voter.
The activity to vote online at WeThePeople is only available to United States Citizens that are met with eligible criteria to vote online.

So, by what means is this Information collected?

Upon utilizing the registration web form, the information collected to the corresponding
text fields; First Name, Last Name, Government ID, Social Security Number, and Password.
Social Security Number requires electronic approval from the registering user.

What will it be used for? Who gets to access it?

The collected personal information will only be used to verify with local or state government to authorize the registering user the ability to vote online at WeThePeople.
Only System Administrators are allowed to make accessible changes to other registered voters at WeThePeople.

Upon registration completion, consent to collect and use the information is given to WeThePeople.
The user has no available options to opt-out or prohibit such collection and use.

The website is hosted with the vendor Salesforce.com, inc.
The information used by this vendor will only be used to verify voter status with local & state government databases.

This site, WeThePeople, does not use any sort of analytics.
If analytics ever became apparent, WeThePeople will be verbose on informed or provided directions to disable any analytical tracking associated with the website.

The collected information stored will only be held until the end of every completed election period.

My Information is protected, right?

Yes, there are upmost security standards and safeguards in place to protect your personal information while visiting WeThePeople.
From end-to-end server and database connection, there is an SSL encryption activated upon data transfer.
SSN and password handling in use of 2,048-bit RSA encryption for safe processing and storage.
Before registration you will be informed by electronic approval that this site is intended to handle your confidential information.

What happens if the privacy statement changes?

This Privacy Statement was last revised on November 15, 2020.
We may change this Privacy Statement at any time and for any reason. We encourage you to review this Privacy Statement each time you visit the web site.

If we decide to make a significant change to our Privacy Statement, we will post a notice on the homepage of our web site for a period of time after the change is made.

What about privacy on other web sites?

This web site may contain links to other web sites.
Some of those web sites may be operated by third parties such as local or state government.
We provide the links for your convenience, but we do not review, control, or monitor the privacy practices of web sites operated by others.

We are not responsible for the performance of web sites operated by third parties or for your business dealings with them.
Therefore, whenever you leave this web site we recommend that you review each web site's privacy practices and make your own conclusions regarding the adequacy of these practices.

How to contact WeThePeople

If you have questions or comments about this Privacy Statement, please contact us privacy@WeThePeople.com.
